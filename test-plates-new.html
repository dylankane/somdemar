Gambas A La Plancha

Succulent prawns grilled to perfection, seasoned simply with sea salt and a drizzle of olive oil. This classic Spanish dish brings the flavors of the Mediterranean straight to your table.

Enjoy the smoky aroma and the tender texture, best paired with a crisp white wine and fresh bread to soak up every last drop of flavor.

Pulpo a la Gallega

Tender octopus slices drizzled with olive oil and sprinkled with paprika, served over a bed of potatoes. A Galician classic that highlights the flavors of the sea.

History of the dish

Originating from the region of Galicia in northwest Spain, this dish is a staple at local festivals and gatherings, celebrated for its simplicity and taste.
